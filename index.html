Question 02
The stylist bios connected to Instagram: how does that work and is it set up once or ongoing?

This is a one-time setup in Phase 1. Each stylist gets their own dedicated page on your website. That page is written and structured to rank in Google search for queries like "[stylist name] haircuts" or "[stylist name] colorist." It also pulls from their Instagram automatically using a live feed integration, so the page stays fresh every time they post without any extra work from your team.

Once it is live, it maintains itself. We update bios as needed (new hires, changes in specialty) as part of the ongoing Phase 2 program.

The connection to social media strategy you asked about: yes, those stylist pages become the foundation for the Phase 3 social program. The content created in the shoots links back to the bio pages, the bio pages reinforce AI search signals, and together they create a loop that drives both discoverability and bookings.

Question 03
What is included from an SEO, AEO, and AI search optimization perspective?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is everything that helps Google find, understand, and rank your website. This includes the technical structure of your pages, the keywords and content on them, how your site is linked internally, and how many credible external sites reference you. This is the foundation.

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is the layer that gets you into AI-generated answers specifically. When someone asks ChatGPT or Gemini a question, the AI pulls from pages that are formatted to be quoted: clear questions and answers, structured data (schema markup), and content that is written to inform not just to rank. We build this into your site in Phase 1 and maintain it in Phase 2.

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the ongoing strategy of building your presence across the web so that AI systems have enough evidence to recommend you confidently. This means citations in local directories, mentions in relevant publications, and a content footprint that tells AI you are the authoritative answer for "best salon in [your market]."

All three are included in the $1,500 per month Phase 2 program. Phase 1 builds the foundation for all three.
